The Project is being produced at Unity and consists of a third-person game of the Survival Horror genre, with a focus on the script.
A somewhat complex idea, focused more on something cinematic
, with Cell Shaded style.
The game is still in its early stages of development,
because I still lack the knowledge and resources to finish it.

//Objects
The character of the game (found in the image inside the folder
Project_ThirdPerson) is being sculpted in zbrush but is not ready yet,
so I'm using a capsule,
I also downloaded a character to train the animations,
and it can be found on the map with a really bad animation
(I'm still a beginner with everything).
The gun can be found in the front, but it still doesn't have a model,
so I'm using a rectangular block.

//Controls
W,A,S,D - Move the character.
Shift - Run.

// Shaders
The water and clouds were made with ShaderGraph
(following some tutorials).

// Textures
The texture of the house was made in the substance designer,
as well as the texture of the streetlights and the texture of the terrain.
The texture of the vegetation was made by hand
Photoshop Portable (however it is still incomplete).
The street texture was only edited in Photoshop Portable,
but it is not yet definitive.
